## Welcome

**TheWay** is a professional web platform designed to help users understand their skills, compare their profile with market opportunities, and identify the skills they need to improve.

The project focuses on a skills-first approach instead of a traditional job-search experience. It helps users make better decisions about learning, career growth, and opportunity selection.

## Project Vision

TheWay connects three important elements:

- User skills
- Market needs
- Professional opportunities

The main objective is to provide a clear, intelligent, and user-friendly platform that supports career development through data-driven skill matching.

## Main Modules

- User account management
- Profile and skills analysis
- Opportunity discovery
- Intelligent matching system
- Skills recommendations
- Admin panel
- Analytics and reporting
- Support and settings
