# FAQ

## What is TheWay?

TheWay is an intelligent skills matching platform that helps users compare their skills with professional opportunities and receive recommendations for improvement.

---

## Who can use TheWay?

TheWay can be used by students, developers, job seekers, freelancers, and professionals who want to improve their career opportunities.

---

## What is the main goal of TheWay?

The main goal is to help users understand their current skills, identify missing skills, and find opportunities that match their profile.

---

## Does TheWay replace job platforms?

No. TheWay is designed to improve the job search experience by focusing on skills and compatibility, not only job listings.

---

## What is the matching score?

The matching score is a compatibility indicator between the user skills and the skills required by an opportunity.

---

## Does the platform use AI?

The platform is designed to support future AI-based features such as advanced recommendations, CV analysis, and learning path suggestions.

---

## Is sensitive information included in the Wiki?

No. Public documentation should not contain passwords, tokens, API keys, database credentials, or private configuration details.

---

## Can the Wiki be updated later?

Yes. The Wiki should be updated regularly as the project evolves.
